Alerts

Alert are created with the .alert class, followed by a contextual color classes:

Primary! Indicates a important action.
Secondary! indicates a slightly less important action.
Success! This alert box could indicate a successful or positive action.
Information! This alert box could indicate a neutral informative change or action.
Warning! This alert box could indicate a warning that might need attention.
Danger! This alert box could indicate a dangerous or potentially negative action.
Dark Grey! Dark grey alert.
Light! Light Grey alert.

Closing Alert

Rhe button with class="btn-close" and data-bs-dismiss="alert" is used to close the alert box.

The alert-dismissible class aligns the button to the end.
